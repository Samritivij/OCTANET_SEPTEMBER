Since 2014, our With Love Foundation has been

— conducting chaxrity fundraisers;
— helping cover the tuition fees of children coming from orphanages and boarding schools;
— providing an opportunity for individuals, companies and organisations to help and support orphans by buying or donating clothes, toys, improving family groups and much more;
— actively participating in various field trips with children from orphanages.
—Our young and dedicated volunteers know how to create a real sense of celebration for children;
— sharing not only material resources, but also the warmth of their hearts.

We strive to do good for children deprived of their main support – their families!
